03.  Projects

David Attenborough Text 2 Speech AI

#PyTorch #CUDA #CloudLambda

I took Nvidia's implementation of Tacotron2 to train a deep neural network over 20 hours of labeled audio transcript data of David Attenborough to generate speech audio in very high likeness of his voice.

If you can't believe me, could you ever imagine him saying the following things below?

In order to generate his voice model for speech synthesis, I need to collect and clean a large amount of voice data that is clean, audible, and free of foreign noise artifacts. A lot of quality audio sample of David Attenborough's voice can be found in AudioBook which can be extract as audio sources.

In order to generate audio transcripts of the audio samples, I used PyDub in order to detect silence long enough in the audio samples to split over into smaller audo pieces. I used QuartzNet model from Nvidia's NeMo Conversational AI toolkit to transcribe the audio speech into text.

Training a deep neural network is something that can't be rushed easily unless you have a large amount of capital for large GPU cloud servers. I trained it on a 4-GPU instance at Lambda GPU Cloud for $1.50 per hour. The training took 4 days to finish.
